Beef Stir Fry

How to Cook Flavorful Beef Stir Fry

Beef stir fry is a classic Asian-inspired dish that is both delicious and easy to make. The recipe typically consists of thin slices of beef, vegetables, and a flavorful sauce that is stir-fried together in a wok or skillet. To make beef stir fry, start by slicing the beef into thin strips and marinating it in a mixture of soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil. Then, prepare the vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, and broccoli, and set them aside. Heat a wok or skillet over high heat, add some oil, and stir-fry the beef until it is browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the wok and stir-fry the vegetables until they are crisp-tender. Add the beef back to the wok, along with the sauce, and stir-fry everything together until it is well coated and heated through. Serve the beef stir fry over rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal. This recipe can be easily customized to your taste preferences by adjusting the seasoning and vegetables used.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup beef broth or water
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the sliced beef, soy sauce, minced garlic, minced ginger, and sesame oil. Let it marinate for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  3. Once the pan is hot, add the marinated beef to the wok and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.
  4. Add another tablespoon of vegetable oil to the wok and stir-fry the sliced bell pepper, onion, and broccoli florets for 2-3 minutes, until they are crisp-tender. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and beef broth (or water) until the cornstarch is dissolved. Stir in the oyster sauce.
  6. Add the beef back to the wok and pour in the cornstarch mixture. Stir-fry everything together until the sauce is thickened and heated through.
  7. Serve the beef stir fry over rice or noodles, garnished with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.
