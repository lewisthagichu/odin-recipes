How to Cook Delicious Air Fryer Pork Chops

Air fryer pork chops are a quick and easy meal to make that are perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. The air fryer is a great way to cook pork chops because it creates a crispy and golden crust on the outside while keeping the inside tender and juicy. To make air fryer pork chops, start by seasoning the pork chops with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Then, preheat the air fryer to 380°F and place the pork chops in the basket. Cook the pork chops for 10-12 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are fully cooked and the internal temperature reaches 145°F. Serve the pork chops with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes. This recipe is a great option for anyone who wants a delicious and healthy meal in a short amount of time.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 (5 ounce) boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a flat shallow dish; mix well.
  3. Coat each pork chop with olive oil. Dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture and set on a plate.
  4. Place 2 chops in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes; flipping halfway through cook time. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining chops.
  5. Served hot and enjoy!
